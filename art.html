Art Events


Get to Know Your Community!


We at Green City believe that an impact in our environment starts with an impact in our community. The art events that we provide will not only bring the community together, but also help our community expand on their creative side. All of our events will take place in the outdoors for the most part because we want our participants to truly be immersed in their environment. With events ranging from children's hand painting to family sculptures; we want our community to get to know each other and build strong bonds in order to eventually collaborate to create greener cities!




Events

Express Yourself

Express Yourself is a program designed to bring the children in our community together. Our youth will engage in activites including: painting/hand painting, collages, landscape drawing, and much more!
These activities are special in the sense that we will incorporate environmental awareness in some form whether it be painting your surroundings or using nature (leaves, pine cones, etc.) to create a collage.
These activites will help our youth develop their skills in self reliance, communication, collaboration, critical thinking, problem solving, creativity, and innovation.
All ages are welcome of course but, be prepared for EXTREME fun!

*Food and drink provided*

This event takes place every Sunday from 12pm to 2pm at Alley Pond Park.



Family Fun Time

Family Fun Time is a program designed to bring the families in our community together. Parents and their children have the opportunity to build stronger bonds and families also have the opportunity to meet other families and build new bonds.
Families will engage in activities including: team sculptures, team face painting, and much more!
These activities are special in the sense that we will incorporate environmental awareness in some form whether it be painting your surroundings or using nature (leaves, pine cones, etc.) to build a sculpture.
These activities will help our families develop their skills in communication, collaboration, critical thinking, problem solving, creativity, and innovation.
All ages are welcome!

*Food and drink provided*

This event takes place every Sunday from 2pm to 4pm at Alley Pond Park.
