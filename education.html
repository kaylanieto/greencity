Educational Workshops


Get to Know Your Environment!


We at Green City believe that in order to impact our environment, we need to first be educated about it. This can made possible through the many educational workshops that we will provide! The workshops will teach participants how to lessen their carbon footprint and make more eco-friendly choices.




Events

Green Cleaning

Green Cleaning is a workshop where participants will learn which common house hold ingredients can be used to keep their homes healthy, clean, and safe. During the workshop, participants will make their very own product (secret!) to take home to use themselves!

*Food and drink provided*

This event will take place from 6pm to 8pm at the New York Public Library on Saturday 12/15/18.


Brighten Up!

Brighten Up is a workshop where participants will learn about light pollution and learn about what steps they can take to reduce it.

*Food and drink provided!*

This event will take place from 5pm to 6pm at New York Public Library on Saturday 12/22/18.
