Sporting Events


Get to Know Your Community!


We at Green City believe that an impact in our environment starts with an impact in our community. The sporting events that we provide will not only bring the community together, but also help our community stay active and healthy. With events ranging from children's soccer to a family foot race; we want our community to get to know each other and build strong bonds in order to eventually collaborate to create greener cities!




Events

Together Stronger

Together Stonger is a program designed to bring the children in our community together. Our youth will engage in activites including: soccer, volleyball, frisbee, baseball, and much more!
These activites will help our youth develop their skills in self reliance, communication, and collaboration.
All ages are welcome of course but, be prepared for EXTREME fun!

*Food and drink provided*

This event takes place every Saturday from 12pm to 2pm at Alley Pond Park.


Family Fun Time

Family Fun Time is a program designed to bring the families in our community together. Parents and their children have the opportunity to build stronger bonds and families also have the opportunity to meet other families to build new bonds.
Families will engage in activities including: team foot races, team bike races, team tag, and much more!
These activities will help our families develop their skills in communication and collaboration.
All ages are welcome!

*Food and drink provided*

This event takes place every Saturday from 2pm to 4pm at Alley Pond Park.
