Current eco-friendliness in NYC

Vertical Gardens

Multiple locations in NYC have vertical gardens, such as Union Square and 152 Elizabeth Street. They change colors with the seasons, improve air quality, and are pleasing to look at.

Empire State Building

The Empire state buildings have insulated windows and a micro-targeted temperature control system which cuts energy use by a third.

Brooklyn Childrens Museum

The Brooklyn Childrens Museum uses solar generated electricity, recycled rubber flooring, and geothermal heating and cooling. The museum also teaches kids about ecology.
